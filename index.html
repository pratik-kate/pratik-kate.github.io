Connections
Connections is a beautifully-crafted, social discovery app built with SwiftUI and Firebase.
It features a polished onboarding and authentication flow, dynamic swipable cards for browsing profiles, real-time chat and matches, image caching, and secure user-to-user interactions.
  • Home Screen: Swipe through recommended profiles with photos and basic info, tap to view more details.
  • Profile Setup: Smooth onboarding for adding photos, personal info, and interests with live preview.
  • Real-time Chat: Secure, fast messaging with typing indicators, image sharing, and push notifications.
  • Match Discovery: Instant notifications for new matches and mutual interest discovery.
